Barba Negra

A concept showing how a traditional barbershop can feel premium online — attracting new clients, showcasing craft, and filling the booking calendar through bold, masculine design.

Open concept preview Request something similar

What this concept proves

A barbershop website does not need to look like a free template. Dark tones, sharp typography, and clear service cards can turn a local shop into a destination brand.

What stands out

Dark charcoal palette with warm amber accents, service cards with pricing and duration, WhatsApp booking integration, and a gallery that sells the atmosphere before the visit.

Who this direction fits

Barbershops, grooming studios, tattoo parlors, and any craft-focused service business that wants its online presence to match the quality of its work.

How a real client could use this direction

This build works best when a barbershop has built a loyal local following but still relies on walk-ins and Instagram DMs. The online booking system alone typically increases appointments by filling gaps in the schedule that would otherwise stay empty — especially midweek slots.

Could include

  • Service catalog with clear pricing and duration
  • WhatsApp booking integration
  • Gallery showcasing interior and work quality
  • Google Reviews integration for social proof
  • Location with hours and Google Maps
  • Fully mobile-responsive design
