About Carlos Barbería

Welcome to Carlos Barbería, a traditional barbershop in the heart of Barcelona's Eixample neighborhood. Since 2007, we have been providing quality barbering services to our clients. Our experienced barbers specialize in classic haircuts, beard grooming, and traditional hot towel shaves.

We pride ourselves on using quality products and delivering professional service. Whether you're looking for a fresh cut or beard maintenance, we're here to help.
