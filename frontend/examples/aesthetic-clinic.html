Philosophy

“We don’t change who you are — we reveal your best self.”

Dr. Elena Vidal

Founder & Medical Director

At Velara, we practice aesthetic medicine as a discipline of restraint. Every intervention is governed by a single principle: less is always more. Our clients come to us not for transformation, but for refinement — the quiet confidence that comes from looking exactly like yourself, only rested, balanced, luminous.

Founded in 2014 by Dr. Elena Vidal, our practice draws on over 12 years of facial anatomy research and a deep belief that aesthetic medicine should be invisible. We work in micro-corrections — movements measured in fractions of a millimeter, results measured in how you feel when you catch your reflection unguarded.

Lead Specialist

Dr. Elena Vidal

MD, MSc Dermatology · Barcelona

Board-certified in dermatology and aesthetic medicine with over 12 years of clinical experience. Dr. Vidal trained at Hospital Clínic de Barcelona and completed advanced fellowships in facial anatomy at the University of Paris. She is known for her conservative approach and belief that the best work is the work no one notices.

She lectures internationally on micro-volumetric techniques and has contributed to peer-reviewed research on minimally invasive facial rejuvenation protocols.
