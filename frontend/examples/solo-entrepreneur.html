How I Work

A clear path from
idea to impact.

01

Understand

Deep-dive into your business, users, and market. I interview stakeholders, audit existing systems, and map the competitive landscape. No assumptions, only evidence.

02

Define

Synthesize research into a product brief with clear scope, success metrics, and a phased roadmap. We align on what "done" looks like before a single pixel moves.

03

Build

Rapid iteration in two-week cycles. Design and code move together. You see working software every Friday, not slide decks. I write the code myself and own the output.

04

Launch & Learn

Ship to real users, instrument everything, and iterate based on actual behavior. I stay engaged post-launch to optimize, not just hand off and disappear.
