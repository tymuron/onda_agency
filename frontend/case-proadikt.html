Real case · Online store

From Vinted bans to a store that looks like a big brand.

PROADIKT was selling boots through Vinted and Instagram. We built his whole online store. In the first weeks: around 2,000 EUR and customers from other countries buying on their own.

01The problem

Before: a lot of hassle, few sales.

He sold mostly through Vinted and Instagram. Banned accounts, reach he could not control, and full days answering messages only to close a handful of sales. His own summary: bans, few sales, wasted time.

02What we built

We built his whole store.

A large, organized catalog by brand, player, league and sole type. Product pages with sizes, shipping and returns made clear. His own cart and secure checkout. Worldwide shipping. Real reviews on the home page. All built so the customer can buy without asking anything.

03After

Now people buy on their own.

New customers from other cities and countries, who did not know him before, buying without messaging him for everything. And a store his own customers compare to the big brands in the sport.

“It looks like the typical big-brand sites, like Futbolemotion or ProDirectSoccer.”
PA customer, on seeing the store

PROADIKT founder: “The design and the look, plus all the info the customer needs.”
