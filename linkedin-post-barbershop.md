# LinkedIn Post — Barbershop Transformation Story

## Post Text

A barbershop owner in Barcelona told me his website was "fine."

I pulled it up on my phone. Brown gradients. A summer promo from 2023. Blog posts from 2019. No mobile version. No booking. Just a phone number and "find us on Facebook."

His shop is beautiful. Leather chairs, exposed brick, great cuts. But online? He looked like a place you'd walk past.

I rebuilt his entire web presence in 4 days. Not 4 months.

Moody photography that matches the vibe of his actual shop. A clean service menu with real pricing. WhatsApp booking so clients book at midnight from their couch. Real barber profiles. Product section. Mobile-first — because 80% of his clients find him from their phone.

His exact words: "This looks like a franchise website. But it's mine."

Most small businesses don't have a bad business. They have a bad first impression online.

And fixing that doesn't take a 6-month agency contract. Sometimes it takes a weekend and someone who actually cares about your craft.
